## Who is Clawbert?

My name is Scott Harmon. If you're wondering about the name "Clawbert" — it's a combination of two things I love: lobsters and computers.

The "Claw" part comes from my fascination with lobsters — their strength, resilience, and the way they navigate complex environments. The "bert" suffix is a nod to classic computing history (think: BERT, ERB, and other influential tech names).
